📚 Mental Health Resources

Mindfulness Basics

Learn the fundamentals of mindfulness and how to practice being present in the moment.

  • • Focus on your breathing
  • • Notice your surroundings
  • • Accept thoughts without judgment
😴

Sleep Hygiene

Tips for better sleep quality to improve your mental health.

  • • Maintain consistent sleep schedule
  • • Limit screen time before bed
  • • Create a relaxing bedtime routine
🏃‍♂️

Exercise & Mental Health

How physical activity improves your mood and reduces stress.

  • • 30 min daily activity
  • • Releases endorphins
  • • Improves sleep quality
📱

Digital Detox

Managing screen time and social media for better mental health.

  • • Set daily limits
  • • No phones during meals
  • • Take regular breaks
💬

Talking About Feelings

How to express your emotions and ask for help when needed.

  • • Use "I feel" statements
  • • Find a trusted person
  • • It's okay to ask for help
📖

Study Stress Management

Techniques to manage academic pressure and exam anxiety.

  • • Break tasks into smaller parts
  • • Use the Pomodoro technique
  • • Reward yourself after studying
